About StudyMate AI

StudyMate AI is an intelligent study companion designed to transform the way you learn from documents. Simply upload any PDF — textbooks, notes, research papers — and our AI engine powered by Google Gemini will help you extract knowledge instantly.

Ask natural language questions and get precise answers drawn directly from your document. Generate multiple-choice quizzes automatically to test your understanding. Track every question you've asked and every quiz you've taken with full history and analytics.

Built with Angular 21, Spring Boot 4, and secured with JWT authentication — StudyMate AI keeps your study data private, organized, and always accessible.
